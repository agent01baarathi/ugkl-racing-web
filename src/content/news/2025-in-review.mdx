# FPV Drone Racing in Malaysia – 2025 Review

What an awesome year it has been for UGKL. We are grateful for the journey we shared with every pilot, volunteer, and supporter who made 2025 one for the books.

## The Numbers

- **11 events** hosted throughout the year
- **International pilots** from Korea, Japan, China, Hong Kong, Singapore, Thailand, and Australia
- **Summer GQ** became the largest MultiGP Global Qualifier ever held in Malaysia
- **CloudLink** won the 2024 Anugerah Inovasi Teknologi Sukan Kebangsaan award

## Highlights

### Spring Global Qualifier
Our Spring GQ saw strong local competition with Vynn taking the top spot followed by AMKFPV and AK2. The event showcased the growing skill level of Malaysian pilots on the global stage.

### Summer Global Qualifier
The biggest event in our history. International pilots from across Asia-Pacific came to compete, with Yuki from Japan taking the Open class and guanLM from China winning Pro. This event put Malaysian drone racing on the map.

### One Last Round
We closed the year with a bang — FLOWERPIG from Korea dominated both Open and Pro classes, while local pilots showed incredible improvement throughout the season.

## Looking Ahead to 2026

We have an exciting calendar planned for 2026 with more events, more classes, and more opportunities for pilots at every level. Whether you are a seasoned racer or just getting started, there is a place for you at UGKL.

Thank you to everyone who made 2025 unforgettable. See you on the track!
