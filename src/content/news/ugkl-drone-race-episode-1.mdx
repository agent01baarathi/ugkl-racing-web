# UGKL Drone Race – Episode 1

What was supposed to be a small community gathering at our usual training ground turned into something far greater. The UGKL Drone Race Episode 1 brought together pilots from across Malaysia and even drew international competitors.

## The Event

The race was held at our regular training venue, which we transformed into a competitive racing circuit. With RotorHazard timing, CloudLink live streaming, and full MultiGP integration, the event ran with professional-grade production quality.

## Results

CJFPV from Korea took first place, showcasing incredible speed and precision. AMKFPV and Isya, both from Malaysia, rounded out the podium with strong performances.

1. **CJFPV** 🇰🇷
2. **AMKFPV** 🇲🇾
3. **Isya** 🇲🇾

## What We Learned

This event proved that the Malaysian FPV community is ready for bigger things. The turnout exceeded expectations, the racing was intense, and the community spirit was incredible.

For many new pilots, this was their first taste of competitive racing, and the feedback was overwhelmingly positive. The combination of experienced racers mentoring newcomers while pushing each other on the track is exactly what UGKL is about.

## What is Next

Episode 1 set the bar high. We are already planning more events with bigger tracks, more classes, and opportunities for pilots at every skill level. Stay tuned and follow us for updates on upcoming races.
